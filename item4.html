Ramen

Recipe from Matthew C. Paraiso

Ingredients

Ingredient Measurement
Broth (Chicken, Pork, Vegetable) 2 cups
Ramen Noodles 1 pack
Soft-Boiled Egg 1
Pork Belly 2-3 slices
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
Sesame Oil 1 tbsp
Green Onions (Sliced) 1-2
Optional: Nori, Corn, Bean Sprouts As much as you desire

Instructions

  1. Prepare the broth: Heat your broth in a pot, adding soy sauce and sesame oil for flavor.

  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the ramen noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.

  3. Assemble the ramen: Place the noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth over them.

  4. Add toppings: Add slices of chashu, a soft-boiled egg, and any other toppings you desire.

  5. Garnish: Sprinkle with sliced green onions for added freshness.

  6. Serve: Enjoy your flavorful bowl of ramen!
